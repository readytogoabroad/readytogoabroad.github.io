About Us

In 2018, two college students have started a special 2-daycollege admission program called “Study Abroad Workshop” to work with a group of Thai high school students who areinterested and have high potential in succeeding in attending colleges and universities in the U.S.A. The program was found on the basis of providing resources to low-income students with a high level of ambition, rigorous commitment to community services, and good academic standing. In June 2020, thecurrent committees have come together to the idea of not only sharing what we know f rom the college admission experience but also providing online English classes that cover basic high school English curriculum to the university level of reading and writing. Our Students Are Hualeem Students






Ready to Study Abroad Program is found and supervised under The Gifted and Talented Foundation or Hualaem, which is a non-profit foundation in Thailand with the goal of supporting gifted and talented students. The Hualaem Foundation supports more than 250 students per year. Located in Chiang Khong, these bright students coming f rom all over Thailand participate in the camp organized by the foundation during school breaks. Our Ready to Study Abroad team members are alumni f rom the foundation. We hope to use the skills and knowledge we have learned f rom the foundation and give back to the students who are eager to learn and develop themselves in highereducation. The students in our program are selected f rom Hualaem Foundation as not only we wish to support the Thai local students but also contribute to being givers who give back to the place we come f rom.

Sponsor Us



In the very early steps in applying to a university, each year, the high school senior students need funds to support their TOEFL ($140 each) and SAT fee ($109 each, including international fees). These are requirements for all internationalapplicants and the students participating in this program are facing tremendous financial challenges that have closed off the opportunities in pursuing higher education in a foreign country. In order to achieve the goal, this fund will help pay not only the test fees but also the additional costs such as travel tickets, room and board, book supplies, etc., which couldimmensely ease the financial struggles. Please donate as you wish. We would like you to be a part of this small programassisting lower-income students in Thailand for a better future of the world
